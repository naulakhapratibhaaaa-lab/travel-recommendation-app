About Us

We're passionate travelers on a mission to help you discover the world's most extraordinary places.

Wanderlust was founded in 2020 by a group of travel enthusiasts who believed that everyone deserves access to honest, curated travel recommendations. We combine local expertise with passionate exploration to bring you the most authentic travel experiences across the globe.
